Features



The Symphony ST is one of SYM's best-selling models and a top choice among the new generation of urban scooters. With features like full LED lighting, A.L.E.H, large storage space, a front-mounted fuel tank cap, and front and rear disc brakes, the Symphony ST ensures both style and performance.
